The Nissan GT-R is a luxury sports car that has been in production since 2007. The 2024 model of the GT-R is priced at $122,885 and has a standard twin-turbo 3.8-liter V-6 engine that produces 565 horsepower . The car can accelerate from zero to 60 mph in 2.9 seconds . The GT-R has a reputation for its handling, which seems to defy physics . The car comes with e-adjustable Bilstein dampers and Brembo brakes with carbon-ceramic setups for the performance-focused variants . Although the GT-R has been updated over the years, it is still based on the same R35-generation coupe that began hitting dealer lots 15 years ago and quickly earned the nickname "Godzilla" . The car's interior lacks refinement and seems crude and rude by today's standards .
